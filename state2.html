Arunachal Pradesh - Famous Art Forms & Crafts

Ponung Dance

Performed by the Adi tribe, this is an all-women group dance done before the harvest season to thank the gods. It depicts stories of crop growth and cultural heritage, led by a male singer called the Miri.

Ponung Dance

Carpet Weaving

The Monpa tribe is known for weaving colorful carpets with traditional designs, passed through generations.

Carpet Weaving

Tangkha Painting

A Tibetan Buddhist art form with vibrant, intricate religious paintings depicting deities and mandalas on cloth or silk.

Tangkha Painting

Cane and Bamboo Craft

Indigenous tribes craft baskets, bags, hats, belts, and containers using cane and bamboo, with intricate weaving unique to their traditions.

Cane and Bamboo Craft

Mask Making

Used in religious ceremonies, festivals, and dances, masks depict deities, animals, and mythical figures, differing among tribes

Mask Making
